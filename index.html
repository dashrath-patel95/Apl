About APL

The Aanjana Premier League (APL) is an annual cricket tournament that brings together 11 villages from the Choudhary Samaj community (sundha pati). This prestigious event celebrates our rich cultural heritage, sportsmanship, and the unbreakable bond between our villages.

With 16 competitive teams and over 225 players battling for glory, APL has become more than just a tournament it's a celebration of unity, tradition, and the spirit of cricket that runs deep in our Rajasthani roots.
